The best movies of 2023-2024 according to Jacinthe

My top 3 movies of the last 12 months

Dune

Great work of art! Visually stunning and Villeneuve's version is bringing a new light to the story of the books (not only the first one).

Dune 2

After reading the first 4 books by Frank Herbert, I enjoyed the second movie as much as the first one. The actors are even more amazing and the costume of Lady Jessika is amazing.

Everything, Everywhere, all at once

The synopsys of this movie is hard to explain. Anyhow, this was a totally disjuncted movie and I enjoyed watching it for the second time. There was more things to understand after a second viewing.
